OVERNIGHT PACKAGES:

How to send overnight packages:

  • Fill out Yellow Time Inc. Courier Request Form (the only form you need to fill out - get it from your Departmental Administrative Assistant.
  • Put contents in any type of package (Airborne, Fedex, UPS, etc).
  • Between the hours of 9am-4pm, you can leave packages in your out-going mail pickup location.
  • Between the hours of 4pm-7:30pm, packages can be dropped off at one of the Courier Request Drop boxes located outside 4267C, or the 6th or 4th floor Reception Centers
  • Between 6:00 - 9pm, packages can be dropped off at the Time Inc. Reception Center in lobby until 9:00 pm.
  • The Time Inc. Courier service is not to be used for personal use.
