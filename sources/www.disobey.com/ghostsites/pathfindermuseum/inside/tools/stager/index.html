The Stager

  • The stager is is located at: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/cgi-bin/private/stager. Look here to stage via your web browser.
  • Documentation on how to stage files using your web browser are here: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/inside/tools/stager/stager-ui.html.
  • Documentation on the UNIX 'push' client can be found here: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/inside/tools/stager/push.html. Look here to stage via your telnet prompt.
  • Documentation (detailed, but somewhat outdated) on how the stager works can be found here: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/private/production/style/stager.html.
  • Rewritten content can be viewed at http://stager.pathfinder.com. (One day soon, this will be what is put into production).
  • Email your questions and comments on the stager to stager@pathfinder.com.
  • Email your requests for stager access to request@pathfinder.com.
  • Email your requests to do large stages (to add or remove more than 500 files at once) to stager@pathfinder.com.
  • A list of acceptable MIME types are located here: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/cgi-bin/private/stager/doc?mode=mimetypes
  • A list of current stager Error Codes and descriptions is located at http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/cgi-bin/private/stager/doc?mode=errors
  • The Reporter Tool (on content not yet staged) can be found at: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/cgi-bin/private/stager/reporter.html
  • The CMS User Activity Report, which logs the amount of files staged by different individuals, along with the time of their last stage, can be found at: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/private/stager/cms_activity.html
  • A Link Checker Tool (this tool is still in development) which reports on known html links in content is located here: http://cp.pathfinder.com:8080/cgi-bin/private/cms/links.cgi
