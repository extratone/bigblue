ALIASES AND LISTSERVS

SYSOPS | WEBMASTERS

To request a new mail alias or subscribe/unsubscribe to an existing alias, send email to request@pathfinder.com

To subscribe to a listserv, send email to listserv@pathfinder.com and, in the message, type:
SUBSCRIBE listname FirstName LastName
. The owner of the list will then give you access permissions.

To unsubscribe to a listserv, send email to listserv@pathfinder.com and, in the message, type:
SIGNOFF listname FirstName LastName.
The owner of the list will then unsubscribe you.
