VRML 95

The First Annual Symposium on
the Virtual Reality Modeling Language

December 13 - 15, 1995
San Diego Supercomputer Center, San Diego, California

Sponsored by the San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC)

The Virtual Reality Modeling Language (VRML) is evolving as a standard for browsing three-dimensional environments on the World Wide Web. VRML 95 will discuss the future of the VRML standard and general technical issues relating to web-based browsing of three-dimensional data.
