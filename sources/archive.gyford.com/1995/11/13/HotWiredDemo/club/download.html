Although the Webomatic will always be available on HotWired, you may want to download your very own (dial-up users, this means you).

To download your Webomatic, just identify your platform of choice (Mac, NEXTSTEP, etc.) below, and click on the appropriate link. A folder, cleverly called Toolkit, will be transferred to your local hard drive. This folder contains all the HTML files and images that comprise the Webomatic. However, these files have been compressed for easy transfer, and you may need to uncompress them manually.

To use the Webomatic locally, just find the Open File command on your browser's menu bar, and locate the file called index.html, which is included in the Toolkit folder.

If you want to get fancy, you can even customize your toolkit by modifying the HTML files. Add your favorite links; remove the ones you hate; paint your initials on the toolbox lid. Make the Webomatic your own.
