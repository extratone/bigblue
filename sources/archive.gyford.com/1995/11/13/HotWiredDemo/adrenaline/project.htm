About the MuDSLuTs Project....

The MuDSLuTs Project , was conceived and launched in
the spring of 1995 with the following agenda playfully in mind:

  1. Establish an independent electronic base camp serving the interests mountain biking public over the internet.
  2. Advocate for fair and open access of (and to) Country, City and Cyburbia.
  3. Consolidate the diverse information sources available, both on the World Wide Web and generally, for mountain bike enthusiasts on their sport and contribute to that information.
  4. Provide a platform for the exchange of honest and free speech, information, opinion and dissent in a climate unbridled in regard to corporate brown-nosing, club sanctions and political correctness.
  5. Educate regarding to and help identify, value and enjoy our natural resources.
  6. Jazz things up a little on the bike circuits and have some fun doing it.

    MuDSLuTs is a private talent group devoted to the spirit and free pursuit of mountain biking in the U.S.
    and around the world. And, finially,The Project, and it's membership are committed by the common pursuit
    and passion of It's namesake- a lust for dirt.

-"All in all, we'll do our damnest to have a damn good time."
