Getting Around HotWired

Welcome to HotWired's Help section. You can reach the Help section at any time by clicking on the Help icon. Help icons appear at the bottom of every page of HotWired.

Help topics are listed below. You can read Help directly from this list or click the Help button in a specific section and link directly to topics that relate to that section. If a topic refers to more than one section, subsequent references will link back to the original topic.

If you cannot find an answer to your question or a solution to your problem on this page or on our Need Help? page, please send email to support@hotwired.com. (For problems accessing Club Wired, please send email to club@wired.com.)
