The Official HotWired FAQ 3.1

Cruise around HotWired the easy way. If you use Netscape and you're a member, we've created a HotWired Bookmark Plug-in to aid your navigation. Follow the link to the Bookmarks page, and save it as type Source. From your Bookmarks menu, choose the View Bookmarks command. Click on the Import button and choose the page you just saved. Presto! You've got a HotWired navigation menu. When HotWired changes, we'll change the HotWired Bookmark Plug-in and post announcements about it. You can also edit the Plug-in to match your personal preferences. Enjoy!
