Singapore has ambitious plans to become a Southeast Asian hub for information technology. Its "IT 2000" vision calls for every home, school, office, and government ministry to be online by the next century. Most Singapore subscribers now have access to speedy ISDN lines, and the country boasts the distinction of having the world's highest number of email messages sent per capita.
