Chep Lap Kok airport lies 15 miles west of the central business district, but it will be well-sutured to the heart of Hong Kong.

The project involves the construction of 21 miles of expressway, two new bridges - including the longest road-rail suspension bridge in the world - a third tunnel beneath Victoria Harbor, and an Airport Express Railway linking Hong Kong and Kowloon directly to the ground transportation center in the airport terminal.
