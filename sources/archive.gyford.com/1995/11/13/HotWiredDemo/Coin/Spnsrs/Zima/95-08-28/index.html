July 27, 1995
The Beach

Ouch! Hot! Ouch! Hot!

Duncan crashed onto the blanket.

He rolled over and grabbed his blistering feet.

Alexandria lifted her head from the book and chuckled.

"Can you put some oil on my back, Duncan?"

She didn't have to ask twice.

Duncan made little circles of oil on her shoulders.

...and rubbed it in.

"You wanna play some volleyball, Alex?"

He didn't have to ask twice.

They grabbed a couple Zima cans

......and headed for the net.
